Location

This family-friendly Chennai hotel is located by the sea, within 3 mi (5 km) of Kapaleeswarar Temple and Marina Beach. Phoenix Market City and Chennai Trade Centre are also within 9 mi (15 km). Chennai Mandavelli Station is 29 minutes by foot.

Hotel features

3 restaurants, a full-service spa and a bar/lounge are available at this hotel. Valet parking is free. Additionally, a coffee shop/café, a spa tub and a sauna are on-site.

Room amenities

All 316 rooms boast deep soaking bathtubs, and offer free WiFi and 24-hour room service. For a bit of entertainment, LCD TVs come with DVD players, and guests will also find free wired Internet and free newspapers.
