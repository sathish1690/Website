About this property

Park Hyatt Chennai

2 restaurants, a full-service spa and an outdoor pool are available at this hotel. Free WiFi in public areas and free valet parking are also provided. Other amenities include a nightclub, a 24-hour health club and a bar/lounge. All 200 rooms boast deep soaking bathtubs, and offer free WiFi and iPod docking stations. Free wired Internet and 24-hour room service are standard, as are LCD TVs with digital channels.
